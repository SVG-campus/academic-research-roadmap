# Fast-Track Math & Science Prerequisite Roadmap

## 1. Context & Objectives

To pursue advanced research in **Topological-Quantum-Cognitive Causal Synthesism**, publish high-level papers in applied mathematics, and gain admission to premier MS/PhD programs (Stanford ICME, NYU Courant, CMU), you must build a bulletproof formal mathematics and science foundation.

Currently, your transcript contains pre-calculus and light non-lab science. This document outlines where and how to obtain accredited **Science Labs** (Physics with Lab, Chemistry with Lab, Biology with Lab) alongside higher math courses.

---

## 2. Accredited Online Science Lab Providers (Hands-On Home Kits & Virtual Labs)

The following regionally accredited institutions offer **self-paced online science courses with lab components** that ship hands-on lab kits directly to your home or provide proctored virtual labs. They issue standard university transcripts accepted nationwide.

### Option 1: On-Campus at Syracuse University (RECOMMENDED — $0 OUT-OF-POCKET)
- **How it works:** Re-enroll at Syracuse University and take **PHY 211 / 221 (General Physics I with Lab)** or **PHY 212 / 222 (General Physics II with Lab)** directly on campus.
- **Accreditation:** Syracuse University (MSCHE).
- **Cost:** **$0 out of pocket** (100% paid for by your Syracuse need-based financial aid package!).

### Option 2: UNE Online (University of New England — Science Prerequisites)
- **How it works:** 100% online, self-paced General Physics I & II with Lab (PHYS 1010/1010L), Chemistry with Lab, or Biology with Lab. Hands-on lab kits shipped to your door.
- **Accreditation:** NECHE (New England Commission of Higher Education — Gold Standard Regional Accreditation).
- **Cost:** **$475 per credit hour** + $45 registration fee (~$1,900 per 4-credit course with lab).
- **Pace:** Complete in **4 to 12 weeks**.

### Option 3: Portage Learning (Geneva College)
- **How it works:** Self-paced online physics, chemistry, and biology with integrated video/virtual labs.
- **Accreditation:** MSCHE (Middle States Commission on Higher Education via Geneva College).
- **Cost:** **~$800 - $900 total** per 4-credit course with lab.
- **Pace:** Complete in **4 to 8 weeks**.

### Option 4: UND (University of North Dakota) Enroll Anytime Physics
- **How it works:** PHYS 203 (College Physics I) or PHYS 251 (University Physics I with Lab). Hands-on lab kits provided.
- **Accreditation:** HLC (Higher Learning Commission).
- **Cost:** **$432.45 per credit hour**.
