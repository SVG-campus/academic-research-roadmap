# Fast-Track Math & Science Prerequisite Roadmap

## 1. Context & Objectives

To pursue advanced research in **Topological-Quantum-Cognitive Causal Synthesism**, publish high-level papers in applied mathematics, and gain admission to premier MS/PhD programs (Stanford ICME, NYU Courant, CMU), you must build a bulletproof formal mathematics foundation.

Currently, your transcript contains pre-calculus and non-lab science. This document outlines the accelerated, accredited online path to complete all required math and physics courses in parallel with your degree and venture operations.

## 2. Accredited Fast-Track Online Course Providers

The following self-paced, regionally accredited university programs offer transferrable college credit that appears on standard transcripts as regular campus courses:

1. **University of North Dakota (UND) Self-Paced Enroll Anytime**
   - Regionally accredited (Higher Learning Commission).
   - 100% online, complete exams via ProctorU.
   - Courses: MATH 165 (Calc I), MATH 166 (Calc II), MATH 265 (Calc III), MATH 266 (Diff Eq), MATH 307 (Linear Algebra).

2. **University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) NetMath**
   - World-class mathematics department credit.
   - Mathematica-based conceptual learning.
   - Courses: MATH 220 (Calc I), MATH 231 (Calc II), MATH 241 (Calc III), MATH 257 (Linear Algebra with Computational Applications), MATH 285 (Diff Eq).

3. **SUNY Online (Empire State / Stony Brook)**
   - Fully accredited, low cost per credit.
   - Offers online Physics I & II with virtual/at-home lab kits.

## 4. Standardized Testing (SAT / ACT / GRE Strategy)

### Undergraduate SAT/ACT
- **Target Institutions:** Stanford Transfer.
- **Requirement:** If applying to Stanford undergraduate, an SAT/ACT score (Target: 1520+ SAT / 34+ ACT) provides a strong boost.
- **Strategy:** Dedicate 2-3 months of self-study (Khan Academy + Official Practice Tests) focusing on Math (aiming for 780-800).
- **Alternative:** Skip SAT/ACT entirely by prioritizing **Columbia GS, Syracuse, or NYU Gallatin**, which do not mandate test scores for non-traditional transfers.

### Graduate GRE General & Math Subject Test
- **GRE General:** Often waived for applicants with top-tier research publications or co-authorships (e.g., with Prof. Gunnar Carlsson).
- **GRE Subject Test in Mathematics:** Strongly recommended for applicants transitioning from non-math undergraduate degrees into elite PhD programs in Applied Math or CS.
