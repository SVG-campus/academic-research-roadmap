## 2. Accredited Online Math Course Details (UND vs. UIUC NetMath)

### UND (University of North Dakota) Self-Paced Enroll Anytime
- **Cost:** **$432.45 per credit hour**.
  - 4-credit Calculus I / II / III = **$1,729.80** per course.
  - 3-credit Linear Algebra = **$1,297.35** per course.
- **Pace:** Fully self-paced. Can be completed as fast as you pass proctored exams (typically **4 to 8 weeks** per course).
- **Do you need them to apply?** 
  - **Syracuse:** **NO**. You do NOT need to spend out-of-pocket money on UND/NetMath before re-entering Syracuse. You can take Calculus I and Linear Algebra right on campus at Syracuse, **100% covered by your Syracuse financial aid package**.
  - **NYU / Columbia Transfer:** Not strictly required to apply, but having Calc I completed strengthens a STEM transfer portfolio.

### UIUC NetMath
- **Cost:** **$433 per credit hour** (+ $3/cr online fee).
- **Pace:** Self-paced (Mathematica-based). Takes **8 to 12 weeks** on average.
