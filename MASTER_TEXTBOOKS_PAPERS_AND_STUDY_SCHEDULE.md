### B. Differential Geometry, Kähler Manifolds & Monge-Ampère Equations
1. **Riemannian Geometry** — *Manfredo P. do Carmo* (Birkhäuser).
2. **Principles of Algebraic Geometry** — *Phillip Griffiths & Joseph Harris* (Wiley-Interscience) [Kähler geometry & Fubini-Study metrics].
3. **Complex Geometry: An Introduction** — *Daniel Huybrechts* (Springer).

2. **Linear Algebra I & II (MAT 331, 531)**: *Linear Algebra Done Right* — Sheldon Axler (4th Ed., Springer).
3. **Real Analysis I (MAT 511)**: *Principles of Mathematical Analysis* ("Baby Rudin") — Walter Rudin (McGraw-Hill).
4. **Modern Algebra I (MAT 601)**: *Abstract Algebra* — David S. Dummit & Richard M. Foote (3rd Ed., Wiley).
5. **Probability & Statistics (MAT 521)**: *A First Course in Probability* — Sheldon Ross (10th Ed., Pearson).
6. **Differential Equations (MAT 485)**: *Elementary Differential Equations and Boundary Value Problems* — William E. Boyce & Richard C. DiPrima.

### E. Physics & Chemistry Sequence
1. **General Physics I & II (PHY 211, 212)**: *Physics for Scientists and Engineers* — Raymond A. Serway & John W. Jewett (10th Ed.).
2. **General Chemistry I (CHE 106)**: *Chemistry: The Central Science* — Theodore L. Brown, H. Eugene LeMay et al. (15th Ed.).

### F. Computer Science & Software Engineering Sequence
1. **Algorithms & Data Structures (CIS 351, 675)**: *Introduction to Algorithms* (CLRS) — Thomas H. Cormen, Charles E. Leiserson, Ronald L. Rivest, Clifford Stein (4th Ed., MIT Press).
2. **Discrete Mathematics & Logic (CIS 375)**: *Discrete Mathematics and Its Applications* — Kenneth H. Rosen (8th Ed., McGraw-Hill).
3. **Automata & Computability (CIS 473)**: *Introduction to the Theory of Computation* — Michael Sipser (3rd Ed., Cengage).
4. **Operating Systems (CIS 486)**: *Operating System Concepts* — Abraham Silberschatz, Peter B. Galvin, Greg Gagne (10th Ed.).
5. **Functional Programming (CIS 252)**: *Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs* (SICP) — Harold Abelson & Gerald Jay Sussman (2nd Ed., MIT Press).
